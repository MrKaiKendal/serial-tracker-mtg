Submit a Mox Jasper

Use this page to report a serialized Mox Jasper sighting. Use the button below to submit your serialized Mox Jasper with your name, serial number, proof link, notes, and a card image.

What to Include

Please include the serial number, your name or preferred display name, a clear image of the card, and any proof link if available.

The best photo shows the full card and makes the serialized number easy to read.

Photo Tips

Use good lighting, avoid glare, and make sure the serial number is not blurry. If possible, take the photo straight-on instead of at an angle.

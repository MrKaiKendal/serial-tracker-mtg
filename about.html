About the Tracker

About This Project

The Mox Jasper Serialized Tracker is a community-built project made to document known serialized Mox Jasper cards from #001 to #500.

What Counts as Confirmed?

A serial number is marked confirmed when there is clear proof that the card exists and the serial number can be verified.

Good proof can include a clear card photo, marketplace listing, grading certificate, social media post, or another public source.

What Information Is Tracked?

The tracker may include the serial number, submitted name, proof link, card image, status, and notes about the card.

Owners can submit as much or as little public information as they are comfortable sharing.
